Caring for the Sick and Bringing Hope to the Lost

Help us to fill hospitals need in Tunisia by providing free or minimal cost medical treatment to impoverished people in remote areas while also sharing the Good News of Jesus Christ with their patients. Yet because these hospitals operate with relatively limited resources, they require financial assistance to make important upgrades or replace aging buildings. Some have simply outgrown their facilities and seek to offer more hospitals, whether it be the addition of patient beds, giving the laboratory a facelift, or installing diagnostic equipment.
